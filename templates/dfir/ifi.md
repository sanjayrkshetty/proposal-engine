## Scope of Services — Internal Forensic Investigation

SISA will conduct a thorough forensic investigation to establish the facts, reconstruct the attack timeline, and provide {{CLIENT_NAME}} with the evidence needed to remediate, report, and recover.

**In Scope:**
- System memory and disk forensics (compromised endpoints and servers)
- Log analysis and correlation across SIEM, firewall, and application logs
- Attack chain reconstruction (initial access → persistence → lateral movement → impact)
- Data examination and classification (PII, financial, regulated data)
- Evidence collection under chain of custody for potential legal proceedings

**Deliverables:**
- Executive summary report (board/regulator ready)
- Technical forensic findings report
- Attack timeline visualisation
- Evidence chain of custody documentation
- Remediation roadmap (short, medium, long term)
