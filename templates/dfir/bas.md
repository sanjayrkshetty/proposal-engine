## Scope of Services — Breach and Attack Simulation

Proposal Engine will execute a structured simulation of attack techniques aligned to the threat actors most relevant to {{CLIENT_NAME}}'s industry and region. The exercise validates whether your security controls detect, alert, and respond as designed.

**What Proposal Engine will do:**
- Design a simulation scenario based on your threat landscape
- Execute MITRE ATT&CK-mapped attack techniques across your defensive stack
- Validate EDR, SIEM, WAF, DLP, and email gateway detection capabilities
- Conduct a purple team exercise (joint attacker/defender session)
- Score your security maturity on a 1-5 scale per MITRE tactic

**Deliverables:**
- BAS execution report
- MITRE ATT&CK coverage heatmap
- Security maturity score with industry benchmarking
- Control gap analysis
- Prioritised remediation plan
