# Scope Estimate — North American Insurance Corp
**Service:** Quantum Readiness Assessment — Enterprise Tier

## Effort Estimate
| Phase | Days |
|-------|------|
| On-site | 10 |
| Remote | 20 |
| Reporting | 5 |
| **Total** | **35** |

**Indicative Price:** ₹150,000 – ₹250,000 USD

## In Scope
- Quantum computer attack vulnerability assessment
- Current encryption methods and key management review
- Compliance review against NAIC Cybersecurity Model Law, NYDFS Cybersecurity Regulation, and GLBA
- Data encryption vulnerabilities identification and prioritization
- Recommendations for quantum-resistant cryptography implementation
- Key management process review and optimization
- Quantum Readiness Assessment report and roadmap

## Out of Scope
- Implementation of quantum-resistant cryptography
- Key management system deployment
- Ongoing compliance monitoring and maintenance

## Deliverables
- Quantum Readiness Assessment report
- Executive summary and presentation
- Detailed findings and recommendations report
- Roadmap for quantum-resistant cryptography implementation

## Assumptions
- Client provides timely access to necessary data and personnel
- Current encryption methods and key management systems are well-documented
- Regulatory requirements remain unchanged during the assessment

## SLA Commitments
- Initial response: 4 hours
- Containment: 24 hours
- Report delivery: 10 business days

## Commercial Risks
⚠ Scope creep due to unidentified vulnerabilities or complex key management systems
⚠ Regulatory requirements changes during the assessment
⚠ Client's insufficient cooperation or data availability
