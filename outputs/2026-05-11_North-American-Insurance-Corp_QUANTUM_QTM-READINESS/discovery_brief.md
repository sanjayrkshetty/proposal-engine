# Discovery Brief — North American Insurance Corp
**Service:** Quantum Readiness Assessment  
**Recommended Tier:** Enterprise — The recommended Enterprise tier is due to the large size and complexity of the organization, requiring a comprehensive and customized Quantum Readiness Assessment to address its unique pain points and regulatory requirements.

## Pain Points
- Data encryption vulnerabilities
- Key management complexities
- Compliance risks
- Legacy system integration challenges
- Cybersecurity talent gaps

## Regulatory Drivers
- NAIC Cybersecurity Model Law
- NYDFS Cybersecurity Regulation
- Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA)
- Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA)

## Environment Assumptions
- Multi-cloud infrastructure
- Hybrid network architecture
- Variety of cryptographic protocols in use
- Large-scale data storage and transmission

## Top Discovery Questions
1. What is the current cryptographic landscape and algorithm usage across the organization?
2. What are the most critical data assets that require protection against quantum threats?
3. What is the current state of key management and rotation practices?
4. Are there any existing post-quantum cryptography (PQC) initiatives or plans?
5. What are the biggest challenges anticipated in migrating to PQC?

## Urgency Signals
- Recent security breaches or incidents
- Upcoming regulatory audits or compliance deadlines
- Planned IT infrastructure upgrades or cloud migrations
- Emerging competitor threats or market disruptions
