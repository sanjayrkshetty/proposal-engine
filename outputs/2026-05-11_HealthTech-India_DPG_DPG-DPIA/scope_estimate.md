# Scope Estimate — HealthTech India
**Service:** Data Protection Impact Assessment — Enterprise Tier

## Effort Estimate
| Phase | Days |
|-------|------|
| On-site | 10 |
| Remote | 15 |
| Reporting | 5 |
| **Total** | **30** |

**Indicative Price:** ₹800,000 – ₹1,200,000 INR

## In Scope
- Conducting a thorough risk assessment of HealthTech India's data processing activities
- Identifying and evaluating the potential impact of data breaches on patient data
- Reviewing compliance with GDPR, HIPAA, and Information Technology Act, 2000 (India)
- Assessing the effectiveness of existing security controls and mitigation measures
- Providing recommendations for enhancing data protection and mitigating risks
- Evaluating data processing activities related to the upcoming product launch
- Reviewing data sharing agreements with third-party vendors and partners

## Out of Scope
- Implementation of recommended security controls and mitigation measures
- Ongoing monitoring and maintenance of data protection measures
- Audit and compliance services for other regulatory requirements

## Deliverables
- A comprehensive Data Protection Impact Assessment report
- A risk assessment and mitigation plan
- Recommendations for enhancing data protection and compliance
- A presentation summarizing the findings and recommendations

## Assumptions
- Access to necessary personnel, systems, and information will be provided
- HealthTech India will provide timely and accurate information about data processing activities
- The scope of the engagement will not change significantly during the project
- The regulatory requirements and laws will remain unchanged during the engagement

## SLA Commitments
- Initial response: 4 hours
- Containment: 24 hours
- Report delivery: 10 business days

## Commercial Risks
⚠ Scope creep due to unidentified data processing activities or systems
⚠ Delays in receiving necessary information and access to systems and personnel
⚠ Changes in regulatory requirements or laws during the engagement
