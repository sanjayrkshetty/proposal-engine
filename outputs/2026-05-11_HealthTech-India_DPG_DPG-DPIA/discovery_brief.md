# Discovery Brief — HealthTech India
**Service:** Data Protection Impact Assessment  
**Recommended Tier:** Enterprise — The recommended tier is Enterprise due to the high-risk nature of processing sensitive patient data and the need for comprehensive data protection measures to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements.

## Pain Points
- Ensuring confidentiality and integrity of patient data
- Compliance with data protection regulations
- Mitigating risks of data breaches and cyber attacks
- Protecting sensitive patient information from unauthorized access

## Regulatory Drivers
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for international patients
- Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA)
- Information Technology Act, 2000 (India)
- Indian Medical Council (Professional Conduct, Etiquette and Ethics) Regulations, 2002

## Environment Assumptions
- Cloud-based infrastructure for data storage and processing
- Use of mobile and web applications for patient engagement
- Integration with existing healthcare systems and networks
- Presence of IoT devices for patient monitoring

## Top Discovery Questions
1. What types of patient data will be collected, stored, and processed?
2. How will data be protected in transit and at rest?
3. What measures are in place for access control and authentication?
4. How will data breaches be detected and responded to?
5. What is the incident response plan in case of a security incident?

## Urgency Signals
- Upcoming product launch
- Increasing regulatory scrutiny in the healthcare industry
- Recent data breaches in similar organizations
- Growing concerns about patient data privacy
